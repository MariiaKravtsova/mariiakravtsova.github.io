About me

Currently a full stack developer at Quikly, working in Rails, GraphQL/React/Redux. My goal is to be a T shaped developer, where the lower part of the T would be React, and the upper - everything else my curiosity takes me. Some of the ways I stay curious is by building projects, reading books, watching talks, or writing an article on Hackernoon or my blog.

Previously, I have spent time creating applications in React Native, MobX, React and Node. I have been fortunate to work with and be mentored by many smart and thoughtful people, and I try to give back to the community as I can.

In 2013, my business partner and I founded Stripe by Stripe, a technology consulting company for small businesses. I was responsible for design and testing of the projects, after which contractors were brought on board for implementation. From this experience I learned the importance of fostering client relationships and analyzing costs, and I honed my project management skills. The startup was sold in 2015.
