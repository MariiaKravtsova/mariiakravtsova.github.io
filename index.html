About me

I am a computer science student at Western Michigan University in Kalamazoo, Michigan. I work at TowerPinkster as an Information Systems Intern, where I have learned a lot about communication, networks, software support, security and creating inexpensive solutions. For my next adventure, I am ready to dive into the world of software development.

I am familiar with WMU's standard languages such as Java, C#, Ruby, JavaScript, R and C, and I have picked up Python in my explorations on the forefront of Artificial Intelligence. I am actively pursuing different aspects of computer language and applications in my personal projects because there are always cool new things to explore in the field of technology.

In 2013, my business partner and I founded Stripe by Stripe, a technology consulting company for small businesses. I was responsible for design and testing of the projects, after which contractors were brought on board for implementation. From this experience I learned the importance of fostering client relationships and analyzing costs, and I honed my project management skills. The startup was sold in 2015.

I'm a self-motivated worker, with a strong belief that bugs and errors are merely road signs, illuminating the path to my goals.
